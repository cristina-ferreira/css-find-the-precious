Gandalf was shorter in stature than the other two; but his long white hair, his sweeping silver beard, and his broad shoulders, made him look like some wise king of ancient legend.

Child of the kindly West. Some courage and some wisdom, blended in measure. If more of us valued food and cheer and song above hoarded gold, it would be a merrier world.

A grim character, for the most part, laughing only occasionally and, though in a few rare situations inspiring amusement, never joking. He also seems to have a love for food.
